# Privacy Policy

> **Effective Date: June 26, 2026**

**Channy Studio** (the "Company") values the privacy of users in connection with the **"RISE (dream) - Mission Alarm"** (the "App") service and complies with applicable personal data protection laws. This policy explains what personal information the Company collects, why it is collected, how long it is retained, and whether it is shared with third parties.

---

## Article 1. Personal Information Collected

The Company may collect the following information to provide the service.

- **Location data**: Approximate current location (GPS or network-based), used to provide the weather widget
- **Device information**: Device identifier (Android ID), OS version, device model name
- **Advertising identifier (ADID/GAID)**: Used to provide personalized advertising via Google AdMob
- **App usage records**: Number of alarms set, mission completion history (stored locally on the device)
- **Error and performance data**: App crash logs, performance measurement data

---

## Article 2. Purpose of Collection

- Providing the weather widget service (using location data)
- Providing advertising services and measuring ad performance (using ADID)
- Improving app functionality and fixing errors
- Analyzing service usage statistics

---

## Article 3. Retention and Destruction of Personal Information

Collected personal information is destroyed without delay once its purpose has been fulfilled. However, where retention is required by applicable law, it is securely retained for the required period.

- **Location data**: Destroyed immediately after retrieving weather information
- **Advertising identifier**: Handled in accordance with Google AdMob's policies
- **In-app alarm/mission data**: Retained on the device until the user deletes the app

---

## Article 4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

As a general rule, the Company does not disclose users' personal information to external parties. The following are exceptions.

- **Google AdMob**: Advertising identifier (ADID) is shared for the purpose of providing advertising services. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Legal requirements**: Where lawfully requested by an investigative authority

---

## Article 5. Collection and Use of Location Information

The App collects device location information to provide the weather widget feature. Location data is used solely to look up weather information and is not stored on any server. Users may deny location access at any time in their device settings; doing so will limit the weather widget feature.

---

## Article 6. Use of Advertising Identifier (ADID)

The App displays advertising through Google AdMob, which uses the device's advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID). Users can reset their advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertising under **Device Settings > Google > Ads**.

---

## Article 7. Protection of Children's Personal Information

This App is not directed at children under the age of 14. If it is discovered that personal information of a child under 14 has been collected, it will be deleted immediately. Parents or guardians with questions about this may contact us using the information below.

---

- Request to access their personal information
- Request to correct or delete their personal information
- Request to suspend processing of their personal information
